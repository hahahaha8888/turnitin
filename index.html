What is Turnitin Non-Repository?
Non-Repository means your document will not be stored in Turnitin's database after checking. This protects your assignment from being detected by other students in the future.
How long to receive the report?
Turnitin reports are usually delivered within minutes after payment confirmation. We strive to ensure the fastest delivery possible.
What file formats are accepted?
We accept files in PDF and DOCX formats. File size must be less than 100MB, not exceeding 800 pages, and containing at least 20 words.
Does the check support Malay language?
Yes! Similarity Check supports multiple languages including Malay. Only AI Writing Detection is limited to English documents.
How is the payment method?
We use Touch 'n Go as the payment method. After you send your file, we will send a TnG QR code for easy and secure payment.
Is my data safe?
Yes, completely safe. We use Turnitin Non-Repository, meaning your document is not stored in any database and is not shared with third parties.
